About Us

Yamada Indonesia Motor Manufacturing is a company that manufactures motorbikes. The company was founded on July 6, 1974. The Yamada motorcycle factory began operating in Indonesia around 1969, as an assembly line business, all components were imported from Japan. Yamada Motor strives to realize peoples' dreams with ingenuity and passion, and to always be a company people look to for the next exciting product or concept that provides exceptional value and deep satisfaction.
